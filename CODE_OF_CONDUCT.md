# Code of conduct

CROPS Circle is a community-maintained project. We welcome contributors regardless of age, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity, level of experience, area of expertise, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or any other dimension of diversity.

This applies to all CROPS Circle spaces — GitHub, any chat servers, and real-world spaces where the community is represented.

## Encouraged

- Welcoming and inclusive language
- Respect for differing viewpoints and experiences
- Gracefully giving and receiving constructive criticism
- Calm, professional handling of disagreement
- Empathy and tolerance toward other community members
- Encouraging and amplifying new voices

## Not tolerated

- Physical violence, threats, or encouragement of violence of any kind
- Sexualized language, imagery, or unwelcome sexual attention
- Impersonating others or dishonestly claiming affiliation with an individual or organization
- Trolling, derogatory comments, personal or political attacks
- Harassment of other community members in public or private channels
- Publishing others' private information — physical or electronic — without explicit permission
- Social engineering, scamming, or otherwise manipulating other community members
- Promoting investments, tokens, or projects for personal monetary or non-monetary gain
- Spamming with off-topic content
- Disregarding requests or warnings from maintainers

## Reporting

Most issues will be visible in public channels and can be addressed there. For anything that needs private attention, email **cropscircle@protonmail.com** with as much detail as you can — including specific examples and timestamps.

Depending on severity, violators may receive a warning, a temporary ban, or a permanent ban from CROPS Circle spaces.
